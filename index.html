Welcome to The Emerald Isle

Authentic Irish Pub & Restaurant

Our Story

Founded in 1995, The Emerald Isle brings the warmth and hospitality of a traditional Irish pub to the heart of the city. Our founder, Patrick O'Sullivan, wanted to create a home away from home for Irish expats and locals alike.

We take pride in serving authentic Irish cuisine, pouring the perfect pint of Guinness, and providing live traditional music every weekend.

100% Irish ingredients
